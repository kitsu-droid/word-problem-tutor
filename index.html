About This App

Welcome to the Math Tutor App! This application helps you practice converting word problems into equations and equations back into word problems.

Features include:

  • Parsing word problems with numbers written in words (e.g., "five") and multipliers ("twice a number").
  • Step-by-step solution hints for Word → Equation mode.
  • Equation → Word problem conversion without requiring an answer.
  • A flexible, responsive interface that works on desktop and mobile.
  • Scrollable About page with detailed explanation about the app’s purpose.

This app was designed to be simple, intuitive, and educational. It emphasizes understanding how to move between word problems and algebraic equations, helping learners build strong foundational math skills.
